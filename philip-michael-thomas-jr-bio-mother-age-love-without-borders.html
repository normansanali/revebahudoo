Philip Michael Thomas Jr Bio, Mother, Age, Love Without Borders

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Bravo TV’s Love Without Borders is quite an interesting show as it features several young men and women who have failed to find their love in their home country. As a part of this social experiment, the participants travel to a foreign country to establish a connection with some special person. Philip Michael Thomas Jr, one of the stars of Love Without Borders, traveled all the way to Ghana to meet Carmen, who he believes is the love of his life. Is Carmen all Philip’s been looking for?

Find that out in the article below and also learn about Philip Michael Thomas Jr’s age, job, family, net worth, and more.

Philip Michael Thomas Jr On Love Without Borders

As per Philip’s bio on Bravo TV’s official website, the reality TV star has experienced a lot in the local dating scene and is now determined to find a real relationship. Also, the website tells us that Philip’s been single for 7 months and celibate for 4 in order to clear his mind and create space for the relationship that he believes is just around the corner.

The producers of the show meticulously chose a partner for Philip, and that is Carmen and she just so happened to be in another corner of the world, a big country in Africa called Ghana.

Like any other relationship, the two had their ups and downs. Whether or not will the two be able to make a heartfelt connection is just a matter of time as will see their journey in this season of Love Without Borders.

Besides Philip Michael Thomas Jr, the Season 1 of Love Without Borders features four other young participants, namely Aaron Motacek, Danna Richards, Naeem Thompson, and Gurleen Virk, who are hoping to find their perfect match in a country aside from their own.

Is Philip Michael Thomas Jr Dating Anyone?

Besides Carmen, we don’t think Philip Jr. is dating anyone. Anyway, we will soon find out if Carmen will be something special in Philip’s life or just another relationship.

Philip Michael Thomas Jr Father

Philip Michael Thomas Jr’s father is Philip Michael Thomas, an actor, and musician. He is best known for his role as detective Ricardo Tubbs on the hit 1980s TV series Miami Vice. His other notable roles came in Coonskin in 1975 and in the 1976 film Sparkle.

Philip also voiced the character Lance Vance in the video games Grand Theft Auto: Vice City (2002) and Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories (2006).

Love Without Borders star has at least ten siblings through his father, according to TheCinemaholic.

Since his birth, the reality TV star was adored by legends like The Marley’s and Betty Wright.

Who Is Philip Michael Thomas Jr Mother?

Philip Jr’s mother is Dhaima Matthews, an international reggae artist. Dhaima was born to John and Susie Matthews as their only child.

Sandra showcased strong musical ability from a young age. She wrote her first song at 9 and by the time she was 12, she would walk boldly into the offices of Tree Music Publishing and tell them she was ready to make a deal.

She was briefly a member of The Buttons, a singing group that consisted of Sandra and her friend, Connie Burns. Some of Dhaima’s popular songs include Ina Jah Children, Reggae on Sunset, Don’t Feel No Way, A True, and Sweat Til Your Body’s Wet.

Philip Michael Thomas Jr Age

At the time of this writing, Philip Jr’s age was 36. He celebrates his birthday on September 20th and shared birthdays with one of the hardest working men in showbiz, @ComedianBamm.

Philip Michael Thomas Jr Job

Bravo TV describes Philip as a “musician on the rise.”

How Much Is Philip Michael Thomas Jr Net Worth?

As of 2022, Philip Michael Thomas Jr’s net worth was reported to be under $400 thousand.

Born to a musical family, Philip had a different connection with music from a young age. Even during his school days, Philip participated in different musical events. Under the stage name PMT, he regularly participated in musical theatres, choirs, and stage productions.

Philip completed his undergrad studies at Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University and while he was there, he secured a deal with Move Sumthin’ Productions, AKA The Exclusives, and started making a mark in the music industry.

Then, Philip recorded for several albums and also wrote for different artists under the guidance of his childhood mentor, Betty Wright. Note that, he was also a member of The M.O.S.T., Betty’s original songwriting group.

At this point in his life, Philip changed his stage name from PMT to Philip Michael. He, then, learned guitar and piano to expand his musical talents. This secured him a deal with Jimmy Rosemund of Czar Entertainment. As a part of this deal, Philip got the chance to work with the legends of the music industry such as Akon, Tank, and Shakespeare.

On March 27, 2012, Philip released his debut album called Box Theory, in partnership with Snatch Digital Entertainment. The album featured songs like Undiscovered, Ay Yi Yi, Free To Be ft. AKON, Like Visa, Hold On, and others. A year later, Philip released a single called Christmas Tonight and in 2019, a single called Follow.
