What Does It Mean When You Hold Up 4 Fingers?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 8, 2024

What does it mean when you hold up 4 fingers? 4 fingers up means you are madly in love. 

The hand gesture is used as an expression of loyalty to your loved one. Nonetheless, the sign can be used as a symbol to represent many other meaningful messages.

While some may use it as a simple pose to convey love, others utilize it to send important signals. From footballers, rappers, and young boys to victims of kidnapping and mental health advocates, everybody uses this sign.

To involve in this trend, one needs to tuck in their thumb and put the remaining fingers of their hand up in the air. Often referred to as a 'silent signal', it is believed to be originated around 2013.

There are over 9 million views on TikTok with the title mentioned above. While there are several meanings for the thumb-down pose, youngsters seem to be using it to show how much they love their girl.

Then, they show the screenshot of its meaning. It is a clever way to say something without actually saying it. So, it is quite popular among youth.

The Sign Has Different Meaing In the world of Rappers

Well-known rappers like DaBaby, Dr.Dre, Tupac Shakur, Snoop Dogg, and many others have clicked photographs making this gesture. It means a Gang sign of them.

According to an article in The Sun, it is a Florida-based sign which indicates 'hood' or 'block' of residents from Broward County in America. 

Social media may have revitalized the hand gesture, but rappers have been doing so for decades.

Footballers use the Hand Sign to Signal the Commencement of the Fourth Quarter of the Game

Playing football requires continuous strength and stamina as it typically lasts for 90 minutes. The last 15 minutes of the game hold great significance as it determines your victory.

As per Distractify, the sign is used in the field to remind players to stay frosty and put their best foot forward. 

People Struggling with Mental Health use the Gesture to Create Awareness

The trend of taking pictures with the sign was used extensively around 2017 to bring awareness regarding mental health issues. 

It was more of a campaign where elite figures like Tom Hardy, Kristen Bell, Louisa Johnson, and Ella Henderson participated.

Meanwhile, many used it as a way to ask for help in dangerous situations like kidnapping or hostage situations. ABC 7 reported that a teenager from North Carolina who was kidnapped used the sign and she was rescued.

The Viral Dude Meme

Dude holding up 4 fingers meme started circulating online in April 2022. A teenage boy was edited into the character Beast Boy, and memes were made using the picture.

Beast Boy is a comic book character from Teen Titans who is green in color. So, the guy was photoshopped green to fit into the character.

All the internet users explored their creativity and elevated the virality of the beast boy meme. It went viral as a TikTok trend in June 2022.

Comedy skits were made, which were hilarious encouraging other people to try them as well. In the skit, other comic characters would ask something to the Beast Boy, and he has to answer in numbers.

The image of Beast Boy showing the sign is used to respond to the question. In this way, there are thousands of comedic materials related to it, which have been viewed millions of times.

In one meme posted by @EraserofCannons, a boy named Robin is sad that their rake is broken. Beast Boy comes to the rescue with his hand which has been photoshopped to look like a rake, with the addition of 3 more.

Similarly, @StarmanSweg uploaded another meme where a cancer patient asks him how many days she has, and Beast Boy replies with his iconic pose.

How did the Beast Boy Meme Trend Start?

A young teenager with the username @freebandtez  posted a photo on his Instagram handle with the sign in 2021. Little did he know it would become a viral meme after a few months.

In October 2021, the first meme using his picture was created and gradually became popular on TikTok with resharing and reposts.

The original meme post has been deleted, but internet users continue to make fun content out of this image and make people laugh.
