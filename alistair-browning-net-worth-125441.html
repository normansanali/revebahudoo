About

Veteran of the Power Rangers franchise who has provided his voice for the Power Rangers' spin-offs Ninja Storm, Dino Thunder, S.P.D. and Dino Super Charge. He is also known for his role as Damrod in the The Lord of the Rings film franchise.

Before Fame

His first taste of entertainment was as an intern at The Mercury Theatre in Auckland, New Zealand in 1977. He played Hamlet at the Court Theatre company in 1980.

Trivia

He was cast as Judge Matkovic in the TV series documentary American Playboy: The Hugh Hefner Story.

Family Life

He was born Alistair James Browning in Dunedin, New Zealand.

Associated With

He starred alongside Marton Csokas in the 2001 movie Rain. He was named Best Supporting Actor in a Feature Film by the New Zealand Film and Television Awards for his role in the film.
