American actress, Odette Annable is enjoying her lavish life with her husband and daughter.

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Actress previously recognized as Odette Yustman who played a lead role in J.J. Abrams' 2008 sci-fi thriller film, Cloverfield is none other than Odette Annable. Some of her numerous credits for television include appearances on Supergirl, Two and a Half Men, Life on Mars, House, and Monk.

If you want to know detailed information about Annable and her personal life we suggest you read the entire article. 

Bio & Early life 

Odette Annable was born in Los Angeles, California on May 10, 1985, as Odette Juliette Yustman. Her mother belongs to Cuban, Lydia (Nodarse) ethnicity. Odette's father was born in Bogota, Colombia, of Jewish descent from Italy and Poland, Victor Oscar Yustman which makes her of a mixed ethnic group. 

Annable attended Woodcrest Christian High School in Riverside, California for her studies and graduated from the same college. She played the leading role in movies like Reckless Behavior: Caught on Tape and Cloverfield. 

Ever since she started off her career, she has been gaining an immense popularity. Not to forget the fact that she also has worked along with popular actor Arnold Schwarzenegger who is also an ex-husband of Maria Shriver, Katie McGrath, Kristen Bell and many more.

Husband: Dave Annable

In the world where people tend to keep their personal life out of the limelight, Odette has always kept her personal life in front of the media without hesitation. Annable is a married woman. Yes, guys, the gorgeous actress is married to the love of her life, Dave Annable.

First Encounter

As per some of the source, the pair met when she starred with him on Brothers & Sisters (2006) during a five-episode personality arc. Right after their first encounter they hit it off and began dating soon. 

Wedding Details

Annable wore a white strapless wedding gown with a veil that made her look stunning. On the other hand, her husband wore a black tuxedo. The couple looked absolutely cute together while exchanging their wedding vows.
