Zachary Gordon also known as Zachary Adam Gordon is an American actor and model. As of 2023, Zachary Gordon’s net worth is $2 million. He is known for portraying Greg Heffley in the comedy drama family film “Diary of a Wimpy Kid” (2010) and Emmett in the comedy film “American Pie Presents: Girls’ Rules” (2020).

He is the recipient of the Young Artist Award for Best Performance in a Feature Film – Supporting Young Actor (2008) and Young Artist Award for Best Performance in a Feature Film – Young Ensemble Cast (2011).
