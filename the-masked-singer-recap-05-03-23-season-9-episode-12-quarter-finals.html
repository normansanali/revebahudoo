The Masked Singer Recap 05/03/23: Season 9 Episode 12 Quarter Finals

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Tonight on FOX The Masked Singer airs with an all-new Wednesday, May 3, 2023, Season 9 episode 12 and we have your The Masked Singer recap below. On tonight’s The Masked Singer season, 9 episode 12 called “Quarter Finals,” as per the FOX synopsis, “It’s the Quarter Finals on The Masked Singer, and the British are coming!

Watch the final four compete for a spot in the coveted Semi-Finals, performing hit songs from artists across the pond. Only one will be unmasked! ”

So make sure to bookmark this spot and come back from 8 PM – 9 PM ET for our The Masked Singer recap. While you wait for the recap, make sure to check out all our television news, spoilers, recaps & more, right here!

Tonight’s The Masked Singer begins now – Refresh Page often to get the most current updates!

In tonight’s Masked Singer episode, its British Invasion night with the four finalists taking the stage to compete. Host Nick Cannon introduces the panel of celebrity detectives; Nicole, Jenny, Robin and Ken. The Top 4, The McCaw, The California Role, Medusa and the UFO will all sing. Three singers will move on with one going home.

The McCaw is up first. He chooses to sing an Elton John song. The audiences and the panel sing to cheer him on. After the McCaw finishes singing, a butler comes to the stage with a clue – a leader. The McCaw shares that he has also been a leader. Ken thinks this is McCauley Culkin. The panel laughs. Robin thinks this might be a professional artist. Jenny thinks Ryan Cabrera. Nicole thinks maybe it is Michael Archaleta.

The California Role takes the stage next and starts off with a famous song that is very intense. He sings I Wish I Was Special with a chorus behind him and a full light show. After a wild performance, they receive a clue. The clue is that the group received a standing O while they performed at The White House. Ken thinks this is the Miami Sound Machine. Jenny thinks the Catatonics.

Medusa is up next. She loves performing with a mask on because her regular job requires her to look a certain way.

UFO takes the stage with an upbeat song. Several British Guards dance and twirl colorful umbrellas. The butler brings out a clue. Nicole thinks this is Emily Collins. Ken believes it is Rebecca Romaine. No one boos him. The audience across likes the guess. Robin thinks this could be Molly Sims.

Medusa takes the stage. She sings an Adele song, moving the panel and the audience. They scream with excitement after she finishes. They love her, and start chanting her name. Nicole thinks she is the one to beat. A clue is brought to the stage. She is a mom. Robin thought this was Kesha but it cannot be now. Nicole thinks maybe Amy Lee. Jenny thinks Halsey. Nicole doesn’t think so.

Its time for one of the four to go home. Nick urges everyone to vote. The totals come in within seconds. The performer that will go home is……UFO. They are very upset to see her go. But they cannot wait to see who this is. The panel reviews their guesses; Robin think Molly Sims. Jenny thinks GiGi Hadid, Nicole thinks this is Emily in Paris while Ken thinks Rebecca Romain.

Thee audience chants for the UFO to reveal herself. It is….Olivia Culpo. Everyone is shocked. They didn’t know she could sing. They chant UFO to end the show after commending her for her energy.

THE END!
