What Is Theo Fennell Net Worth? Jewellery Designer Hefty Career Earnings

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 2, 2024

The British jewellery designer Theo Fennell’s net worth is estimated to be in the millions.

Theo Fennell is a jewelry and silverware designer whose unique and beautiful jewellery designs have captured the attention of not only people but of big celebrities like Sir Elton John and David Furnish.

He has been in the jewellery business for about forty years and is still going strong and is one of Britain’s leading fine jewellery & silverware companies.

What Is Theo Fennell Net Worth 2022?

The net worth of Theo Fennell is estimated to be about $3 million to $5 million. He has been in the jewelry business for four decades and has made great money from his business.

He seems to be a pal with the legendary singer, Sir Elton John, as he was seen wishing him on his birthday. His jewelry company not only makes rings and necklaces but brooches too which were worn by celebrities at various events like EE British Academy Film Awards and EJAF Academy Awards.

Theo started his career by being an apprentice and designer at Edward Barnard, a well-established silversmith. Slowly he moved to work solo in a small studio and workshop opposite his working place.

He moved to Fulham road and opened a small shop with a studio and workshop attached to it. Later, a show gallery was made just below the workshops and studio where various exhibitions occur.
