Karin Nanami (Japanese: 奈波果林 Nanami Karin) is a Japanese voice actress for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

As an adult, Nanami became a voice actress and was involved with a few anime series. She became involved with the Pokémon anime during Pokémon the Series: XY, voicing Team Flare scientist Celosia as well as Lucianne, one of Baraz and Meray's childhood friends from Arche Valley. In addition, Nanami voiced Elinn, a character in Pokkén Tournament.
