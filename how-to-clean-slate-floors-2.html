How to clean slate floors

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, July 25, 2024

What is the best cleaner for slate floors?

Damp-mop the floor with a high-quality washable mop and plain water. For occasional deep cleaning, you can also add a few drops of mild detergent, such as dish soap or fabric detergent for delicate fabrics (such as Woolite) to the wash water.

What do you clean slate with?

Regular Cleaning

Mix a few drops of mild dish detergent in a couple of cups of warm water. Use a soft rag (or mop) soaked in the soap solution to rub off dirt and grime. Dry with a towel and let the surface air dry completely overnight.

How do you make slate look new again?

To restore the color of slate tiles, start by cleaning away dust, dirt and other contaminants. Wipe the surface with a dust mop to remove loose particles, then use a wet mop and a cleaner that’s designed for natural stone or slate.

How do I make my slate floor shine?

If you want your slate tiles to have a glossy, polished look, use a topical glossy sealer. You also have the option of a solvent-based or water-based sealer; however, a solvent-based one will last longer and be more durable.

Can I use olive oil on slate?

Squirt a few drops of teak oil, WD40 or olive oil onto a clean, dry cloth. Use another clean, dry cloth to once more buff in an extra shine to the hearth. This will help to absorb excess oil and will accentuate the luster of the slate.

Can you clean slate with WD40?

Day to day cleaning is simply a wipe with a soft damp cloth, and we recommend the use of WD-40 for buffing up the slate if this is needed at any time.

Can you use vinegar on slate?

To keep your slate floors looking their very best… Never use acidic cleaners such as vinegar, which can damage and discolor the stone. Avoid scrub brushes with stiff or metal bristles that may scratch the flooring.

How do you clean unsealed slate?

Mop your slate tile floor with a gentle cleanser. You can use a cleaning product specifically made to wash natural stone, or any other cleaning solution that does not contain lemon or vinegar – preferably one that is pH neutral. Rinse with clean water and either let it air-dry or use a soft cloth.

Why is my slate turning white?

Your slate was probably coated with a sealer or color enhancer, and that’s what’s turning white or gray in response to heat. Sealers make stone less porous. Installers are typically the ones who apply sealers and color enhancers after the stone is installed.

Can you steam clean slate floors?

Slate floors can give your home a cool, stylish feel. Use a steam cleaner or steam mop on your slate floor. This is a good way of targeting both tiles and grout in a gentle process that won’t damage your tiles.
