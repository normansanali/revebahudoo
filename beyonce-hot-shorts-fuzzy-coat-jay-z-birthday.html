Beyonc Proves the Perfect Accessory to Itty-Bitty Shorts Is a Massive Fuzzy Coat

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Beyoncé once again stamped her approval on hot shorts, and this time, she declared every pair of micro pants needs a massive, ultra-luxurious fuzzy coat.

Queen Bey blessed her 319 million Instagram followers' eyes on Saturday night when she dropped a caption-less carousel of photos on Instagram from Jay-Z’s birthday party in France, which took place earlier this month. In true Renaissance fashion, her latest look is equally cozy as it is opulent.

The “CUFF IT” singer sizzled in the teeniest-tiniest pair of shimmering silver Prada shorts paired with a sheer turtleneck bodysuit, also by Prada, that revealed her white bra underneath. Ever the maximalist, she upped the ante with a huge luxe fur coat in a light gray hue. The fabulous piece dropped right above her feet, on which she wore white pointed-toe pumps. Beyoncé further accented her gorgeous look with glittery oversized hoop earrings and dramatic cat eye shades. She styled her platinum blonde hair in a half up, half down ‘do complete with face-framing strands.

In addition to snaps of Beyoncé striking power poses in her shining ‘fit, she included a photo of herself sipping on a glass of red wine and one of her beau holding two bottles of Château Pétrus wine, one of which was from the rapper’s birth year, 1969.

This wasn’t the only big milestone Beyoncé celebrated this month—Wednesday commemorated the 10th anniversary of her fifth studio album, Beyoncé. A decade ago, the superstar dropped the self-titled project without any promotion. She sold 828,773 units in the first three days and went on to win three Grammy awards for the project the following year.
