Kelli Giddish Biography

Kelli Giddish is an actress who has appeared in the NBC legal drama Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, the ABC soap opera All My Children, the Fox crime thriller Past Life, and the NBC crime drama Chase. Di Henry on ABC’s All My Children (2005–2007), Dr. Kate McGinn in Fox’s murder thriller Past Life (2010), and Annie Nolan Frost on NBC’s mystery drama Chase (2010–2011) are among Giddish’s previous appearances.

Kelli Giddish Career

Giddish starred as Diana Henry in the ABC serial opera All My Children from 2005 to 2007. On September 19, 2007, it was revealed that she would make her final appearance as Di. The thriller Death in Love, which premiered at the Sundance Film Festival, stars Giddish (2008). She played US Marshal Annie Frost in the NBC police procedural drama Chase, which began in the fall of 2010. After five episodes aired in April 2011, NBC canceled the show in early February 2011. She also portrayed Courtney in the web series Burg and All’s Faire, where she played Cindy. Giddish made an appearance in the 13th-season premiere, “Scorched Earth.” In the film Solving For The Unknowns, Giddish’s character (Amanda Rollins) was promoted to the position of chief of police.

After Chris Meloni’s departure, Ice-T commended her and co-star Nick Amaro, stating they had to “re-roup, like a football team,” after Meloni’s departure. In May 2018, Giddish starred in the music video for Body Count’s “All Love Is Lost,” which featured Soulfly’s lead singer and former Sepultura founder Max Cavalera, Giddish’s fellow Special Victims Unit co-star.

In the episode “Outsider” of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit in January 2007, Giddish played rape victim Kara Bawson. On June 27, 2011, it was announced that Danny Pino of Cold Case and Giddish would join the cast of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit for the thirteenth season, which would coincide with Christopher Meloni’s departure from the program. Giddish told TV Guide that during the summer filming, “Everyone on set is buzzing with excitement and vitality. They’ve lost a family member with Chris Meloni’s departure, but they’ve been fairly tolerant of us. Amanda is as excited as I am to be working with these people.”
