Megan Fox's appearance has fans doing a double take at Halloween party with Machine Gun Kelly

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

The Transformers star, 37, looked frightfully gorgeous

    Megan Fox paid special homage to Quentin Tarantino as she and fiancé Machine Gun Kelly dressed as characters from the film at the Annual Casamigos Halloween Party. 

    Megan, 37, looked iconic dressed as the teenage assassin Gogo, in the character’s classic schoolgirl outfit. The star wore faux bangs with her usual jet black tresses and added a gory twist of blood dripping down her face as an ode to the moment Gogo weeps blood in the film.

    She added a diamond-encrusted meteor hammer hanging across her body as an opulent purse and another nod to the character’s weapon of choice.

    She attended the party with fiancé Machine Gun Kelly, 33, who dressed as The Bride, Beatrix Kiddo, classically played by Uma Thurman. He wore a bright yellow tracksuit covered in blood with his usual platinum blond hair tousled. 

    Also at the party, hosted by Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber, were a myriad of other celebrities. Cindy's daughter Kaia Gerber attended with Oscar-nominated boyfriend Austin Butler, the duo were dressed as Andy Warhol and his former muse Edie Sedgwick.

    At one point Justin Bieber, dressed as a scuba diver with flippers, led dancing in front of the DJ booth singing Despacito.

    Paris Hilton channeled bestie Britney Spears as she wore the air hostess uniform from the Toxic music video to the party.

    Megan and Machine Gun Kelly, meanwhile, were seen holding hands they traipsed through the party, dispelling any previous concerns fans may have had about their relationship following reports that their wedding plans had ‘stalled’.

    Megan denied rumors that her fiancé had cheated on her in February when she took to Instagram to confirm: “There has been no third party interference in this relationship of any kind. That includes, but is not limited to...actual humans, DMs, AI bots or succubus demons.”

    Meanwhile, the star will release a book of poetry on November 7 called Pretty Boys Are Poisonous: A Collection of [expletive] Up Fairy Tales. She described the book as “an attempt to excise the illness that had taken root in me because of my silence.
