Who Is Mariah Scott The Next Level Chef? Everything To Know About The Contestant

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Mariah Scott is a 37 years old professional Chef from Houston, known to participate in Gordon Ramsay’s The Next Level Chef reality show. 

Mariah Scott is a well-established chef, V.I.P. Ambassador of ALTO Ride, and the owner of Lavishly Seasoned which offers services like Group/Private Cooking Classes, Catering, and Knife Skills.

Scott started her career in the beauty industry as a Make-up artist and Esthetician in San Francisco. She even went to become a flight attendant and traveled all over the world for many years.

Furthermore, Mariah’s life changed when her grandmother demised in a car accident when returning home from church. After that incident, she decided to share her food experiences with those who haven’t traveled like her.

So, Scott started a Youtube channel, vlogged her travels and food recipes. People started liking her so she turned her passion into a full-time lifestyle.
