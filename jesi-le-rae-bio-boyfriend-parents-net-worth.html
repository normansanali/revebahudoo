Meet Actress/Model Jesi Le Rae! Her Bio, Boyfriend, Parents, Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Have you seen Amazon’s newest commercial yet? Well, then you might have also seen Jesi Le Rae over it. Yes, she’s the medusa with the glasses!

In case you aren’t petrified by her gaze already, keep reading this Jesi Le Rae Bio to learn more about her.

Jesi Le Rae Plays Medusa In A New Amazon Commercial

Jesi Le Rae appeared on Amazon’s Jan 2022 commercial as “Medusa,” the only mortal Gorgon who’s able to turn those who look at her into stone.

So, the commercial began with Jesi (as Medusa) tired of turning people into stones, until one day she orders a nifty pair of shades from Amazon Prime. Without hesitation, the Olympian then hits parties with her friends and even petrifies one of the bothersome bar visitors who attempts to hit on her group of friends.

Jesi Le Rae Net Worth

Jesi Le Rae garnered a net worth of under $400 thousand by 2022.

This talented actress initially began her career in the showbiz after auditioning for dancers to perform at EDC in 2012. She then moved to LA with only $450 to her name, and soon found herself traveling the world for dance — Dubai, China, Taiwan, Seychelles, and all across the U.S.

Meanwhile, her dance had captured many hearts, it also caught the attention of many photographers who offered her to work. Thus, she fell into modeling. Over time, she then started also receiving offers from top-end productions including HBO, FXX, CBS, Netflix, and she was on a roll.

As of 2022, she’s appeared in over 15 movies, TV shows, and music videos including — Lazarus (2021), Sam Smith & Demi Lovato: I’m Ready (2020), RNP (2019), Ballers (2017), Demi Lovato: Cool for the Summer (2019), and The Weeknd: Often (2014).

Looking back, Jesi shares that her journey wasn’t easy. Her key to success was patience. “While waiting for opportunities, I was creating to expedite what I wanted to show people. I found my supportive circle to keep me focused during the times of fear and doubt,” she explained.

“After all, I have achieved, I still take failures silently,” the star remarked.

But this actress has much more to go. She still challenges herself to be the jack of all trades. No wonder, she’s also stepped up as a director to produce the 2019 horror movie RNP.

The ultimate gold for Jesi would be to end with acting. Whether that’s in Sci-Fi Fantasy films/television or cartoon animation voice-over,  she just aspires to inspire others.

Back in 2019, Jesi was also named one of the top influencers in LA. That year, she also graced the spring edition cover of Playboy magazine.

Does Jesi Le Rae Have A Boyfriend?

Jesi Le Rae appeared to be single as of 2022. But she already has an ideal boyfriend portrayed in her mind. The actress wants someone who loves himself because only then “can he unconditionally love” her.

Also, something that turned her on the most was “authenticity in a human.” She wants her boyfriend to be true to himself, and have his own set of standards and humility.

Furthermore, he shouldn’t have even a drop of ego, because she hates it the most.

As for her sex life, Jesi was happy with it. She only went for “it”, if she believed her partner was “worth it.”

Did you know: Jesi’s all-time girl crush is Audrey Hepburn in everything she represents.

Who Are Jesi Le Rae’s Parents?

Jesi Le Rae’s parents are hard-working immigrants. They were an example for Jesi, “how even without a high school diploma, you can still be successful.”

No wonder, the actress left college just after a year.

Not much was known about her father, but Jesi’s mother Lilia De Leora (birthday: February 5) @confidentwithoutego was a business professional from Mexico. As of 2022, she resided in San Diego, California living her life “free from hate, regret & pain.”

Talking about her siblings, Jesi has a sister named Jazmin Español (birthday: August 30).

Jazmin is a mother, a wife, and a Human Resources Assistant who worked for Mitchell 1. She’s the one with degrees in the family. To mention, she went to Helix Charter High School, Stockdale High, California State University, and studied  Human resources at San Diego State University.
