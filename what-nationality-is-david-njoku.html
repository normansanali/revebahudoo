What nationality is David Njoku?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 4, 2024

David Njoku born July 10, 1996, is an American football tight end for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football in Miami (FL). He was drafted by the Browns in the first round of the 2017 NFL Draft.

What nationality is David Njoku?

Early years. Njoku is one of nine children born to Nigerian parents who immigrated to the United States. He attended Cedar Grove High School in Cedar Grove, New Jersey.
