Shen Yue Social Network

Timeline

In 2020, Shen is set to star in the romance comedy drama Count Your Lucky Stars alongside Jerry Yan.

In 2019, she performed in the annual CCTV Spring Festival Gala, presenting the sketch "Office's Story (办公室的故事)" alongside other veteran artistes. The same year Shen starred in Another Me, a television drama based on the film Soul Mate (2016) alongside Chen Duling.

In 2018, she portrayed the female lead Dong Shancai in the 2018 television series Meteor Garden, based on the Japanese shōjo manga series Boys Over Flowers (花より男子 , Hana Yori Dango) written by Yoko Kamio. The series was a hit in the Southeast-asian region, and led to increased popularity for Shen overseas. She then became a cast member of the Chinese variety show The Inn 2.

Shen made her acting debut in the Chinese television series Autumn Harvest Uprising in 2017, and proceeded to play a supporting role in the science fiction comedy television series Let's Shake It. The same year, she was cast as the female lead in the campus romance drama A Love So Beautiful.

Shen rose to fame with her role as Chen Xiaoxi in the 2017 campus romance drama A Love So Beautiful alongside actor Hu Yitian, based on the novel To Our Pure Little Beauty by Zhao Gangan, which aired on Tencent Video in 9 November 2017.

Shen Yue (Chinese: 沈月 , born 27 February 1997) is a Chinese actress. She is known for her leading roles in the Chinese television series A Love So Beautiful (2017), Meteor Garden (2018) and Another Me (2019).

Shen Yue was born on 27 February 1997 in Wugang, Hunan province of China. She studied Journalism and Communication at Hunan Normal University in 2014. She was also an intern at Hunan Satellite TV. During her second year in college, her photographer friend posted a photo of her online, which attracted the attention of her future agent. She was then selected to participate in the reality show Summer Sweetie broadcast by Hunan Broadcasting System and later became an intern in Happy Camp.
