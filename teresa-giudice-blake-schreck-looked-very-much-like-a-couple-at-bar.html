Teresa Giudice, Blake Schreck Looked Very Much Like a Couple at Bar

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 2, 2024

Out on the town! Teresa Giudice and realtor Blake Schreck enjoyed a fun-filled night in New York City on Thursday, June 27, Us Weekly can exclusively reveal.

The Real Housewives of New Jersey star, 47, and the West Virginia University graduate, 26, were spotted together at R17, the new rooftop cocktail bar at Pier 17 in the Seaport District, where they “sat across from each other on the terrace,” according to an eyewitness.

“Teresa was drinking red wine. Blake was drinking a beer,” the eyewitness tells Us. “They were laughing a lot together. Blake was making Teresa laugh, and she was giggling. They looked very much like a couple on a date.”

Although the pair ended up leaving the bar together at 9 p.m., Giudice apparently wanted to stay longer to watch Charli XCX perform on the roof.

“Blake was eager to get her out of there,” the eyewitness tells Us. “When Teresa came out of the bathroom, she asked Blake if they could stay and he said, ‘I already called the elevator. Let’s get out of here.’”

The Standing Strong author’s attorney, James J. Leonard Jr., meanwhile, tells Us, “This is a working business relationship between two professionals. They are collectively working on a project that includes other individuals and have been for quite some time. It’s 100 percent strictly business.”

The Bravo personality, who has been married to Joe Giudice since October 1999, was first spotted with Schreck on New Year’s Eve. A source told Us at the time that Teresa was “aggressively flirting with” the Totowa, New Jersey, native as he rubbed “her leg in a romantic way” at the Miami hot spot Big Pink. However, her attorney contended that it was simply “a night out with friends, nothing more than that.”

Teresa and Schreck reignited romance rumors in February when they returned to Miami as Joe, 47, served the final days of his 41-month prison sentence for mail, wire and bankruptcy fraud. The duo were photographed holding hands at a black-tie event, having dinner together and hitting the beach during their weekend getaway. Days later, Andy Cohen said on Watch What Happens Live that he wanted the reality star “to be getting it from this guy.”

The fate of the Giudices’ marriage has been up in the air for some time now. In October, a judge ordered Joe to be deported to his native Italy. He has been in the custody of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement since March, when he was released from prison, as he awaits a final decision in the case. Teresa, who served her own 11-month prison sentence for fraud in 2015, announced in January that she plans to separate from her husband if he is deported. The couple share four daughters: Gia, 18, Gabriella, 14, Milania, 13, and Audriana, 9.

Us Weekly has reached out to Schreck for comment.
