Jill Nicolini Biography and Wiki

A Farmingville, New York native, Jill Nicolini is a journalist known for working as a traffic reporter on WPIX 11 Morning News. She is also a former reality television personality, actress, and model. She is currently in a relationship with Peter khachadurian. 

Jill Nicolini’s impressive career in broadcasting has taken her through various roles and stations, showcasing her versatility and dedication to her craft. One of her earlier positions was as an entertainment and feature reporter at Fox News Channel, where she honed her reporting skills and gained valuable experience in the industry.

Her journey continued as she joined WLNY-TV, where she took on the role of a traffic reporter, providing essential updates to viewers about the flow of transportation in the area. In addition to this, Jill also served as a fill-in host for the popular show “Live From The Couch,” where she undoubtedly left a positive impact on the audience.
