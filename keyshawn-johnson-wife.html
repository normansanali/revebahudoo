Keyshawn Johnson Age, Wiki

Former footballer Keyshawn Johnson was born on 22 July 1972. He took birth and was brought up in Los Angles California.

Keyshawn is currently 49 years old. His full name is Joseph Keyshawn Johnson.

Keyshawn was admitted to Palisades High School for his sophomore and junior year and later attended Susan Miller Dorsey High School where played high school football for the Dorsey Dons.

After then he attended the University of South California where played college football and earned All-American honors.

He was chosen by the New York Jets in the 1996 NFL Draft as the first overall pick.
