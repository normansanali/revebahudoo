After recent allegations and controversy, the fans want to know about Colleen Ballinger health. Did she lose weight? Let’s find out. 

Colleen Mae Ballinger is an American comedian and YouTuber. Besides that, she is also an actress, singer and writer.

She is best known for her Internet persona Miranda Sings, which she uses to upload videos to YouTube.

The comedian is also well-known for producing and acting in the Netflix original series Haters Back Off and for presenting her one-woman comedy show in theaters worldwide.

Ballinger has made theater, television, recordings, and web series appearances as an actress and vocalist.

The actress performed as Miranda Sings on Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee with Jerry Seinfeld among other talk shows, and she also played Dawn in the Broadway version of Waitress.

Moreover, she has participated in the web series Escape the Night (2018–2019) and had three appearances on The Tonight Show.

Selp-Helf and My Diarrhe are two additional best-selling books written in Miranda’s voice by Ballinger.
