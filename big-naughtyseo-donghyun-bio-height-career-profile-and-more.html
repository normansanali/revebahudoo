Big Naughty known by his birth name as Seo Donghyun was born on 2nd June, 2003 in South Korea. His zodiac sign is Gemini and he holds Korean citizenship. Talking about his education, he attended Hanyang ES, Daemyeong MS, Daewon Foreign Language High School. He joined H1ghr Music in the year 2019.

Seo Donghyun Career

Big Naughty made an appearance on the rap competition television show titled Show Me the Money 8 where he was seen to start to earn people love and attention. Big Naughty released singles such as “Bada”, “Problems”, “010” and “Astronaut” on the show and finished in 4th position. Then somewhere in October 2019, he had the chance to sign to H1ghr Music.

And in November within the same year, he released a single titled Where It All Started Remix. He also participated in H1ghr Music’s compilation album titled H1ghr: Red Tape and H1ghr: Blue Tape. The albums earned nomination at the Korean Hip hop Awards as Hip hop Album of the Year.
