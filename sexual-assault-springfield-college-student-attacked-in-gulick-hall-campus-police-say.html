Sexual Assault: Springfield College Student Attacked In Gulick Hall, Campus Police Say

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Campus police sent an email out to students and staff about the 1 a.m. attack on Monday, Feb. 13, at Gulick Hall. They asked residents to be vigilant and report anyone who matches the description of the suspect. 

“This afternoon, February 14, 2023, a sexual assault by an unknown male was reported to have occurred at approximately 1:00 AM on Monday, February 13, 2023, in Gulick Hall. Both the Department of Public Safety and the Office of Non-discrimination Initiatives/Title IX have been notified and are actively supporting the survivor.

"The suspect is described as a white male with a mullet-like haircut and some short facial hair, an athletic build, and calloused hands.”

Springfield College campus police ask anyone with information on the assault to contact investigators immediately at 413-748-5555.

Police said they are upping their patrols in residence halls, and officers asked students to be aware of their surroundings and report anything suspicious.
