Wiki/Biography

She was born on Tuesday, 23 August 1988 (age 31 years; as in 2019), in Delhi. Her zodiac sign is Leo. She did her schooling from Mata Jai Kaur Public School, Ashok Vihar in Delhi. Later, she did a bachelor degree in tourism from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). Thereafter, she did years internship with ‘The Oberoi Hotels’ in Jaipur. Later, she worked with ITC Hotels.

Family, Caste & Boyfriend

She belongs to a North Indian Hindu Family. Her father, Shiv Kapoor, is an entrepreneur (owns a furniture export business) and her mother, Dimpy Kapoor, is a marketing executive. Earlier, her mother used to work as a teacher. Vaani has an elder sister, Nupur Chopra, who is married and settled in Dubai.

Career

Vaani started her career as a model with the modelling agency, Elite Model Management. She has worked with renowned fashion designers like Payal Singhal and Sailex Nairangbam. She has also done many TV commercials including Cadbury Dairy Milk Silk, Oppo Mobiles, and Tribhovandas Bhimji Zaveri Jewellery. She debuted as an actor in the 2013’s Bollywood movie Shuddh Desi Romance. In 2014, she debuted in Tamil movie Aaha Kalyanam, which was the remake of Bollywood movie Band Baaja Baaraat (2010).

Vaani Kapoor in the Movie- Aaha Kalyanam

She has also appeared in the Bollywood films Befikre (2016) and War (2019). She was selected as the female lead in Ranbir Kapoor and Sanjay Dutt starrer Bollywood movie ‘Shamshera’ (2020).

Controversy

Just before the release of her second Bollywood movie ‘Befikre’ (2016) she underwent a lip and chin surgery to enhance her looks. Her surgery turned out to be a disaster, and she got badly trolled for it on the internet. However, the actress has denied it. [2]
