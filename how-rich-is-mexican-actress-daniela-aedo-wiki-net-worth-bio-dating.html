Daniela Aedo Wiki Biography

Daniela Aedo Santana, born on the 12th of February 1995 in Mexico City, Mexico, is an actress, guitarist, singer, and songwriter. She gained recognition for her work on Televisa, starring in various children’s soap operas, including the international hit “Angel Face”. Aedo has been active in the entertainment industry since 1998.

The estimated net worth of Daniela Aedo is $13 million, as of early 2013, with television and music being the main contributors to her wealth.

Daniela Aedo Net Worth $13 million

She was raised in Mexico City by her parents Jose Antonio Aedo and Irma Santana. At the age of five, she was selected to star in “Angel Face” (2000 – 2001), and she also won the INTE Award for Child’s Talent of the Year. Following this, she continued to appear in various television series and gained recognition at a young age.

In 2009, she made a return to television with a special appearance in “My Sin” after a period of inactivity. Additionally, she pursued her studies and was accepted at Berklee College of Music in 2014, showcasing her talents as a guitarist, singer, and composer.

She has performed two concerts in Latin America and presented original songs from her album “I Go To You”. In 2018, she returned to television with appearances in the series “As the Saying Goes” and “Four Lives”.

In her personal life, she is known to be dating her college sweetheart Tadeo Kvitca.
