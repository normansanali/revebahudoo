Who is Kat Timpf?

Katherine Clare “Kat” Timpf is an American television personality, reporter, and comedian. She’s best known for her appearances on Fox News Channel, where she has been a contributor and made regular appearances on various shows. Timpf often brings a libertarian perspective to the network’s political discussions and is known for her quick wit and humorous takes on current events.

She was born on October 29, 1988, in Detroit, Michigan. Timpf received her B.A. in English from Hillsdale College in 2010. Before her tenure at Fox News, she worked for other media outlets and websites, including Campus Reform and The Washington Times.

Timpf has also worked as a stand-up comic and has made guest appearances on several television and radio programs. In addition to her media work, she’s an outspoken critic of political correctness and often discusses issues related to free speech and civil liberties.
