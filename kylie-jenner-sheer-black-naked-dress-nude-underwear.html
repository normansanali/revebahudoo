Kylie Jenner Puts an Edgy Spin on the Naked Dress

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Kylie Jenner just put an edgy spin on the naked dress.

The makeup mogul shared a series of photos on Instagram last night of herself lounging on a plush white sofa in a totally sheer black dress. The piece featured long sleeves and a mock turtleneck; and for a more naked look, Jenner layered it over a nude bra and thong set. The underwear blended in with her skin tone and could barely be seen under the dress.

The youngest Jenner wore full bronzy glam and a glossy lip for the photo. In one shot, she is directing a sultry glance at the camera while playing with her hair.

The look seems to be the same one she wore in a string of selfies she shared in May—seemingly for a photo shoot for her Kylie Cosmetics brand. On her Instagram Stories yesterday, Jenner shared clips of herself trying out her new makeup products while wearing the black dress.

Instagram

Jenner loves a see-through look. In May, she stepped out in New York City wearing one of her best sheer looks yet: a spring dress from Jean Paul Gaultier’s ready-to-wear Flowers collection. Daughter Stormi Webster was with her and adorably wore a miniature version of the piece.

Jenner’s sheer beige dress featured a large print of red, orange, and yellow roses. Underneath, she wore only a nude bra, a silky nude corset, and a matching thong—all of which were exposed, due to the dress’s sheer fabric. The mom of two paired the dress with silky, pointy blush heels, a red clutch, a diamond ear cuff, a silver ring, and shimmering rosy makeup. Her hair was styled in an elegant updo.
