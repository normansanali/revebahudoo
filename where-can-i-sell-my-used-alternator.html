Starters and alternators ($2-$10) They are both a great resource for scrap copper and steel, so salvage yards and other places will pay a decent price for them. Many times, these parts can be refurbished to be used again in other cars or they can be broken down for their materials.One may also ask, can you scrap alternators? Alternators should be brought to your local scrap metal yard or auto recycler to be scrapped when they are no longer of use to you. Also to know, are old alternators worth anything? The metal inside is worth some money (copper wiring). Unless there’s damage to the housing or wiring (methinks that’s not very likely) they are rebuildable with not much effort, so they worth quite a bit.
