Princess Eugenie Shares New Adorable Photos of Baby August

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Princess Eugenie is marking her husband's birthday with new family photos.

The royal took to Instagram today to share a series of images featuring her two-month-old son, August Philip Hawke Brooksbank, and her husband, Jack Brooksbank, partaking in some sweet father-son moments. In one image, Brooksbank looks straight into the camera as he cradles his son, and in another, he has the infant snuggled close in a baby carrier, showcasing just a glimpse of his adorable face. The final image is a solo photo of Brooksbank photographed from behind, walking along an array of lush flowers and a glistening pond.
