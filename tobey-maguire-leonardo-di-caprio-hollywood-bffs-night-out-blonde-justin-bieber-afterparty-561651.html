Spider-Man STRIKES OUT! Tobey Maguire can't score even with Leonardo DiCaprio as wingman

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Tobey Maguire and Leonardo DiCaprio have been the best of friends ever since they were teenagers. While the latter seems to be lucky in falling in love and scoring hot ladies way younger than his age, it seems Tobey might still have to work on his charm.
 
The two were recently papped partying in the VIP section of nightclub MainRō restaurant and supper club in Hollywood on Thursday, February 10. And, it looks like the ‘Spider-Man’ star failed to get a date for the night despite Leo acting as his wingman. According to a report by Page Six, a gorgeous lady and the 46-year-old actor were introduced to each other by Leo but they did not click. Sources spilled the beans on the same saying that even with Leo’s help, Tobey could not land a girl. For the unversed, Tobey Maguire was married to jewelry designer Jennifer Meyer from 2007 to 2016. They split after nine years of marriage.

The BFFs- Tobey and Leo had a ball with their second night out this week. Tobey was apparently spotted getting close to a blonde bombshell but the actors left within two hours of their arrival. Tobey was seen rocking a casual outfit just like the ‘Titanic’ star, as reported by Page Six. Leo is already dating 24-year-old actress and supermodel Camila Morrone but Tobey it seems is very much single. However, the blonde was not the only woman at their table. Leo apparently got other ladies too to help his friend out.

Sources close to Page Six stated, “Tobey and Leo came to the club together for a boys’ night out. It was very clear that Tobey was on the prowl and Leo was his wingman. Leo introduced Tobey to three different blondes.”  However, it seems nothing materialized even after Leonardo’s help. “Even with Leo’s help, Tobey couldn’t land a girl,” the source shared adding, “None of them seemed interested when introduced.” According to reports, Leo and Tobey arrived at 10:30 pm but left the venue after two hours “because it was clear they were having no luck.” Tobey and Leo also partied at Justin Bieber‘s after party of his concert at the Pacific Design Center on Friday night, February 11 in West Hollywood, California. Tobey and Leo have together shared the screen space in movies namely ‘This Boy’s Life’ and ‘The Great Gatsby’.
