Ian Hecox Movies and TV Shows  – In this piece you will get to know all about Ian Hecox movies and tv shows.

But then who is Ian Hecox? Smosh’s online comedy enterprise was co-founded by Ian Hecox, who, along with childhood friend Anthony Padilla, writes, directs, and stars in the comedic sketches that they have been producing since 2005.

A lot of people have been inquiring lot about Ian Hecox movies and tv shows and making various research about them on the internet.

This article talks about Ian Hecox movies and tv shows and everything there is to know about them.

Biography of Ian Hecox

Ian Andrew Hecox was born on November 30, 1987, in Fair Oaks, California, USA. Although there is not much information about his childhood and family, his mother Sharon Hecox and his unknown father occasionally appeared in his videos.

Hecox has a sister named Melissa and a brother named Adrian. He met his close friend Anthony Padilla in sixth grade at Del Campo High School in California. After graduating from high school, they both enrolled at American River College in Sacramento.

In 2002, Padilla created a website called smosh.com which Hecox later joined him on. They created a YouTube channel titled ‘Smosh’ in 2005, only five months after YouTube’s launch.

The channel began with funny videos of themselves and rapidly grew in popularity, earning several awards for having one of the highest number of subscriptions. Although the content of ‘Smosh’ changed over the years, it always aimed to make its viewers laugh and have fun.

In June 2017, Padilla left ‘Smosh’ to pursue a personal project, and Hecox took over the channel. As of 2022, ‘Smosh’ has over 25 million subscribers and over 200,000 views per video. Hecox currently collaborates with a group of YouTubers to create great content for his audience.

Ian Hecox Movies and TV Shows

Ian Hecox is primarily known for his work as a YouTuber and content creator on the channel ‘Smosh’, which he co-founded with Anthony Padilla. However, he has also appeared in a number of movies and TV shows, including:

Movies:

  • Smosh: The Movie (2015): Hecox played himself in this comedy film based on the YouTube channel ‘Smosh’.
  • Ghostmates (2016): Hecox played the lead role of Charlie in this comedy film, which was also produced by ‘Smosh’.

TV Shows:

  • The Big What If (2020): Hecox provided the voice of Michael in this animated series.
  • AOK (2018-2019): Hecox wrote and provided voices for several episodes of this animated sketch comedy series.
  • The Goldbergs (2017): Hecox made a cameo appearance as a store clerk in an episode of this sitcom.

It’s worth noting that Ian Hecox’s primary work has been as a YouTuber and content creator, rather than as an actor in traditional movies and TV shows.
