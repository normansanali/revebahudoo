What is the new Lionel Messi documentary?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Lionel Messi, often regarded as one of the greatest soccer players of all time, has achieved numerous milestones throughout his illustrious career. One such significant achievement is beautifully captured in the documentary series "Messi's World Cup: The Rise of a Legend." 

Introduction to the Documentary

The documentary series, released on February 21, 2024, on Apple TV+, delves into Messi's crowning achievement of winning the 2022 World Cup in Qatar with the Argentina national soccer team.

It provides an intimate and unique look at Messi's quest for a defining World Cup victory, offering fans behind-the-scenes access to his experiences, emotions, and challenges throughout the tournament.

Episode 1 - The Last Cup

The first episode of the documentary offers a captivating portrayal of Messi's childhood and early influences, providing viewers with a glimpse into the family and home that shaped his remarkable journey.

It effectively captures the tension of Argentina's defeat to Saudi Arabia, a critical juncture in their World Cup journey, and offers insightful comments from teammates, highlighting their struggles and determination.

Episode 2 - We Can Dream Again

This episode focuses on Messi's role as captain and his team's journey through the World Cup. It effectively contrasts the support system of the current team with the reliance on Messi in previous World Cup campaigns.

The episode beautifully sets the stage for the subsequent matches, emphasizing the team's collective efforts and the burden Messi carried for the nation.

Episode 3 - The Weight of a Nation

The third episode delves into Messi's repeated failures with the Argentina national team and his decision to retire from international football in 2016.

It offers valuable insights from teammates and Lionel himself, providing a compelling narrative of Argentina's struggles and reflections leading up to the 2022 World Cup.

The focus on the match against the Netherlands and the team's escape from failure adds depth to the storyline.

Episode 4 - Redemption

The final episode, though considered the weakest, captures the anticipation and rituals leading up to the 2022 World Cup final between Argentina and France.

It offers glimpses into Messi's experiences and interactions with his teammates, highlighting the emotional significance of the victory and the impact on the nation.

Behind-the-Scenes Insights

The documentary also provides intriguing behind-the-scenes moments, including Messi's approach to the tournament as potentially his last World Cup and his camaraderie with teammates.

Production and Collaboration

The collaboration between Messi and Apple for this documentary began about three months before the tournament, facilitated by Inter Miami managing owner Jorge Mas.

The series expertly weaves together footage of Argentina's 2022 World Cup journey with Messi's previous heartbreaks in international competitions, offering a compelling narrative of resilience and ultimate triumph.

Critical Analysis

While the documentary received acclaim for its immersive storytelling and unique access to Messi's journey, some critics noted missed opportunities, such as the lack of focus on Messi's pre-match speech and certain pivotal moments in the final match, which could have added further depth to the narrative.

"Messi's World Cup: The Rise of a Legend" serves as a captivating portrayal of Lionel Messi's crowning achievement at the 2022 World Cup.

Through its four compelling episodes, the documentary series offers fans a profound insight into Messi's journey, his impact on his team, and the emotional significance of the victory for Argentina.

As the documentary continues to resonate with audiences, it stands as a testament to the indomitable spirit of football as embodied by Lionel Messi, and a celebration of his enduring legacy in the world of soccer.
