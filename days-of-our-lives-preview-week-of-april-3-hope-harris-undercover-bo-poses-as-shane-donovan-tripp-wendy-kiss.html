Days of Our Lives Preview: Week of April 3 Hope &amp; Harris Undercover Bo Poses as Shane Donova

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers preview video for the week of April 3-7 teases that Wendy Shin (Victoria Grace) and Tripp Johnson (Lucas Adams) will finally do something about the attraction they’ve been struggling to contain.

Wendy will admit that something’s happening between them and will share a steamy kiss with Tripp, so he’ll definitely be pleased over this new development.

This is bad news for Johnny DiMera (Carson Boatman), but he can always cry on Chanel Dupree’s (Raven Bowens) shoulder!

Over with John Black (Drake Hogestyn), he’ll treat Marlena Evans (Deidre Hall) to a well-deserved bubble bath after her nightmare on the island with Megan Hathaway (Miranda Wilson).

John will have a romantic evening with Marlena as he pampers his true love.

Other Days spoilers say Stefan DiMera (Brandon Beemer) and Gabi Hernandez (Camila Banus) will come together for some secret passion – and it looks like it’ll actually lead to them making love this time.

Gabi and Stefan didn’t fall for the trap that Nicole Walker (Arianne Zucker) and EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel) set, but that doesn’t mean they’ll give up on reconnecting in a sizzling way.

DOOL’s weekly preview video shows Gabi pouncing on Stefan in the DiMera secret room, so his romantic setup will pay off.

However, Stefan and Gabi had better be ready for trouble since that room really isn’t so secret anymore.

Days viewers will also see Hope Williams Brady (Kristian Alfonso) and Harris Michaels (Steve Burton) go undercover in Greece, so they’ll try to blend in like a happy couple on vacation.

That’ll lead to some kisses and blurred lines as love blooms on this mission.

Next, Bo Brady (Peter Reckell) will tackle an undercover mission of his own as he poses as Shane Donovan (Charles Shaughnessy) and claims he’s the director of the ISA.

Days’ weekly promo shows Bo wearing glasses and a suit as he uses a comically bad British accent, so this should be fun!

Bo will have some other tricks up his sleeve, especially since his brainwashing has messed with his head.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say there’ll be some wild surprises in Greece, so stick with us for updates on all the quests and chaos.

CDL will have other exciting Days of Our Lives spoilers, updates and news, so check back often for more DOOL details.
