Brice Gonzalez Of The EnkyBoyz Speaks Out Following His Fathers Passing

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 12, 2024

Prayers up!

Randy Gonzalez father to Brice Gonzalez, and 1/2 of the popular TikTok duo, the EnkyBoyz, has passed away. Randy was 35 years old at the time of his passing. Randy previously spoke out about his diagnosis with colon cancer. He shared that he only had 2-3 years to live.

Following his passing, his son, Brice spoke out via Instagram. "It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of my father , Randy Gonzalez. My dad fought a long battle with cancer and is finally at peace with our Lord."

The post continued, "I would Like to thank everyone for your kind words & condolences. It really means a lot. We will be doing a public memorial in Houston Tx. More information will be provided at a later date. If you would like to donate please do so by donating on cash app $Bricethadice. If you would like to send any cards or letters please email us."

Brice says he wishes he could hug his father one last time, "I wish I could hug you & give you a kiss one more time dad but I know you will be watching over me, mom and the girls. Until I see you again dad. I promise to make you proud. I love you. Please keep my family in your prayers.."

Father-son duo, The EnkyBoyz, rose to social media stardom after fans were in awe of the beautiful dynamic the two share. Together, they have 15 million followers on Instagram. Brice, 6, also landed a role of George Lopez' Lopez VS Lopez which airs on NBC.

George Lopez shares his condolences' following the passing of Randy. "My sincerest condolences to Brice and the entire Gonzalez family," the actor wrote in a Jan. 25 Instagram post. "The love and the bond Randy had for his son and his family was undeniable, you can see it in every video. He will be missed, but never forgotten. My heart goes out to the entire family."

May he rest peacefully.
