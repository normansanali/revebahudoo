Alix Klineman Husband: Is The Volleyball Athlete Married Or Dating?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 23, 2024

Alix Klineman doesn’t currently have a husband but is dating a loving boyfriend Teddy Purcell. Let’s learn more about the couple below.

Alix Klineman is a former indoor and beach volleyball player from the United States.

Moreover, Alix won the 2006 Gatorade National Player of the Year in high school.

Likewise, she was a four-time All-American in volleyball at Stanford University and was named the national player of the year by Volleyball Magazine in 2010.

Further talking about her career, Alix was a member of the Junior National Women’s Volleyball Team for the United States. She was also a part of the women’s national volleyball squad of the United States too.

Klineman spent two years in Italy playing volleyball for several clubs from 2011 to 2015.

On an interesting note, she retired from indoor volleyball in 2017 to devote her entire attention to beach volleyball.
