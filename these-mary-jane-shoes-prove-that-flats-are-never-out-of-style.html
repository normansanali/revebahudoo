These Mary-Jane Shoes Prove That Flats Are Never Out of Style

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 18, 2024

The Mary Jane shoe is a cultural icon. As a matter of fact, they have been a popular style for more than a century already and are currently one of the hottest spring 2023 shoe trends from the runways while being more polarizing than, say, white sneakers. The adjustable straps make Mary Janes suitable for pairing with many other styles.

Related: 10 Best Shoe Boutiques On Instagram To Spend On

Choose a pair with heels to add a little height and sophistication, or go with flats for all-day comfort. You may dress them up for a formal occasion or rock a more casual look with them. We did the legwork for you and compiled a list of the finest Mary Jane shoes, considering various preferences in stability, comfort, and heel height.

10 Charles & Keith Buckled Mary Jane Pumps - Chalk

Get these pumps that a woman can rely on to look good and feel comfortable throughout the day and the workday at Charles & Keith. The rounded square toe and hammered gold-tone buckle are just two of the many modern details that will set you apart from the crowd. These Mary Janes will make your legs look longer while providing all-day comfort thanks to the low 6 cm trapeze block heels. Having a versatile chalk coating is a plus.

  • Buy From: Charleskeith.com

9 Larroudé Blair Ballet Flat In Black Patent Leather

You can't go wrong with the Blair ballet flat in black, which is a fresh take on the classic Mary Jane silhouette, updated with modern details. Perfect for the months of Fall and Spring, it's crafted from black patent leather that sparkles no matter the light and features an adjustable ankle strap for a secure fit. They look great with tights beneath a short dress or on their own with jeans, a nice top, and a blazer.

  • Buy From: Larroude.com

8 Jeffrey Campbell Reine Platform Mary Jane Pump

Mary Janes, with a substantial platform, like those designed by Jeffrey Campbell, are the footwear of choice for those who want to make a statement. The Reine pump has a 1-inch platform, a 3.5-inch stacked heel, and an adjustable hook-and-loop strap for a customized fit. These high heels were designed with a club girl of the '90s in mind. Compared to the more traditional round-toe silhouette, the square toe adds dimension and modernity.

  • Buy From: Nordstrom.com

7 J. Crew Anya Mary Jane Flats In Calf Hair

These Mary Janes, made from calf hair, are a modern take on a time-honored classic. They are a cut above the rest because of their higher vamp and daring square toe. The luxurious Italian calf hair is meticulously-stitched into sleek new footwear that will go with everything from business attire to jeans and dresses in neutral and bold hues.

  • Buy From: Jcrew.com

6 Gucci Black Horsebit Mary-Jane Heels

Cult classic Gucci Black Horse bit Mary-Jane pumps will have you striding down the like you're on the runway in no time. Made in Italy, the black patent leather uppers are as shiny as they are durable. The Mary-Jane style is classic and sophisticated. Gucci's signature Horse bit hardware is prominently displayed upfront. It boasts a closed square toe, a vamp strap with an adjustable pin buckle, and a big block heel with rubber injection. Stunning gold metal and padded leather insoles complete the look.

  • Buy From: Matchesfashion.com

5 Wayderns 2-Inch Round Toe Low Chunky Heel Patent Mary Jane

These high-end pumps are both understated and enticing. They're constructed out of a light and airy material that makes them look almost see-through, but don't worry—you won't have to sacrifice any of the usual comforts for the stylish look. An ultra-cushioned insole and a durable rubber sole ensure that your feet will be happy in these shoes. The two-inch-tall, clunky heel may make your legs appear longer and taller when you're wearing them.

  • Buy From: Amazon.com

4 Jessica Simpson Selventa Platform Mary Jane Pump

Inject some sass into your outfit with the Jessica Simpson Selventa platform pump. The color blocking and brogue details on this synthetic pump are sure to turn heads. The Jessica Simpson pump has a large platform and a block heel to give you a boost, and the platform is quite thick.

  • Buy From: Nordstrom.com

3 Miu Miu Glitter Block Heel Mary Jane Pumps

This shimmering pair of Mary Jane pumps are Miu Miu's take on the classic shoe style, which the brand is well-known for reinterpreting using a whimsical and creative perspective. The shoes include glitter in silver all over the upper, and they are finished with a front strap ornamented with a chain. The heel of the shoes is in the block form.

  • Buy From: Farfetch.com

2 Dolce&Gabbana Embellished Mary Jane pumps

These showstoppers are a must-have. A cluster of crystal buttons has been added to the top of the strap of the classic Mary Jane pump that was given an opulent makeover by the fashion house Dolce & Gabbana. The design, produced in Italy, is finished with a piece of black technical fabric for a surprisingly soft look and feel, which contrasts with the precision of its decidedly pointed toe.

  • Buy From: Mytheresa.com

1 Jimmy Choo Ade Embellished Suede Ballet Flats

There is no difference in the level of glitz between Jimmy Choo's flats and the label's finest heels. Made in Italy from soft black suede, these square-toed 'Ade' sandals have been embellished with dripping imitation pearls and glistening crystals along their slim straps.

  • Buy From: net-a-porter.com
