Hoda Kotb's hairstyle is completely different in must-see throwback photo see transformation

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024

The 4th Hour with Hoda and Jenna host showed off a Halloween costume inspired by her early reporting days

    The Today Show stars are getting in the Halloween mood already, and among the first costumes they've debuted? None other than dressing up as Hoda Kotb!

    As the spooky holiday fast approaches, over at , hosts Hoda and Jenna Bush Hager offered up some last minute costume ideas, and made themselves the inspiration.

    The two gave their crew and fans some Hoda and Jenna-themed inspo, and NBC contributor Donna Farizan nailed her retro Hoda look.

    Hoda took to Instagram following the fun Halloween segment to give her stamp of approval to her colleague's costume, and had fans raving over the real throwback photo that inspired it.

    The veteran journalist shared an epic photo from her early news reporter days, where she is wearing an oversized red blazer with a black collar and, of course, padded shoulders, and her hair is voluminously coiffed.
