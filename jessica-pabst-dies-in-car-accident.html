Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

We sadly and unexpectedly lost Jessica Pabst on February 8th at the far-too-young age of 43.

She was having a good time, giggling, and enjoying the sunshine in Florida with her devoted family.

Mike, her devoted spouse, is devastated, as are her family and friends. Jess had a kind heart and was a devoted wife, mother, sister, aunt, friend, and partner. She was a writer, a health advocate, a dreamer with goals, and just a lovely, kind, spirited person. She also had a deliciously offbeat sense of humor.

Jess was very concerned about her health and spent a lot of time studying even the tiniest details so that she and her loved ones would be better off.

As demonstrated by her words, laughter, and array of colorful clothing, she was the brightest and most colorful light in any space.

Family and acquaintances were dear to Jess. They and everyone who encountered them are aware of how much Jessica loved her husband Mike and how much Mike loved Jess. Their relationship dates back almost 26 years. They did everything together and shared a unique and imaginative love and relationship that was filled with laughter. They had aspirations and objectives for the future. Her death is utterly devastating.
