Zendaya Arrives at Fendi's Paris Show in a Plum Gown and Micro Bangs

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 29, 2024

Zendaya has brought the glamour not only with her looks this Paris Couture Week, but also with her unexpected hairstyles.

This morning, the Euphoria star arrived at the Fendi Couture Spring/Summer 2024 show in a slicked-back low bun that showed off her ultra-chic, super straight micro bangs. Very couture, very Parisian.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

Zendaya’s reddish-brown hair perfectly mirrored her plum cold-shoulder gown, which featured a thick halter-style band attached to the inside of the top that wrapped around the back of her neck. The floor-length bodycon design by the Italian fashion house also featured opera glove–esque sleeves that extended to Zendaya’s knuckles, with cutouts for her fingers.

Beauty-wise, the Emmy winner opted for a deep burgundy lip with light pink highlighter brushed around her eyelids and contoured on her cheeks. For accessories, she wore a single silver princess necklace, which featured a teardrop pearl dangling from the middle.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Zendaya also showed out in Paris earlier this week, when she arrived at the Schiaparelli show in a striking all-black look. The form-fitting ensemble was composed of a jersey turtleneck top adorned with twisted fabric studs all the way down the sleeves.

The statement top was tucked into a column skirt that featured folds of fabric around the waist and a coiling black skirt that extended behind her, evoking Old Hollywood glamour.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

It was there Zendaya debuted her new chop, which included helmetlike micro bangs topping her straight, long ’do.
